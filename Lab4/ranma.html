Ranma Saotome

Ranma Saotome is the teenage lead character in the manga and anime series "Ranma 1/2". He was created by Rumiko Takahashi, the writer and illustrator of the original manga series.

Background

Ranma Saotome was born in Japan to a Genma Saotome, a martial artist from the school of Anything Goes Martial Arts. As a small child, Ranma was taken by his father to undertake rigorous martial arts training while traveling throughout Japan and China. It is during his training in China that Ranma falls into a cursed spring and turns into a red-haired girl. Ever since then, Ranma turns into a girl whenever he comes in contact with cold water and reverts back to being a boy when he comes in contact with warm or hot water. He has been engaged to the youngest daughter of his father's best friend to inherit Genma's martial arts school.

Relationship with Other Characters

Ranma is not the only person to have fallen into a cursed spring. Other characters in the series who have suffered the same fate as Ranma (though they don't turn into the opposite sex) are the following:

  • Genma Saotome - father - turns into a giant panda.
  • Ryoga Hibiki - rival - turns into a black piglet.
  • Shampoo - female Chinese Amazon - turns into a cat (Ranma hates cats).
  • Mousse - male Chinese Amazon - turns into a duck.
  • Taro - rival - turns into a giant winged minotaur.
